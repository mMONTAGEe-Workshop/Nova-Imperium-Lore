Nova Technocratic Scientific Assembly


The Nova Science Team represents a group of brilliant Imperial scientists and intrepid explorers who have come together under the leadership of the Nova Technocratic Scientific Assembly, which governed under Nova Navy supervision. This esteemed organization was established in the year 3307 as a branch of the Auxiliary in the Nova Navy. Since then, they have garnered significant resources and support from the local factions and Nova Paresa, enabling them to become a self-sufficient faction in their own right, complete with settlements, cities, and starports, all operating under the jurisdiction of the Nova Navy.

Their mission is to delve into the mysteries of the galaxy, unraveling the secrets of the vast emptiness of space, and discovering new knowledge that can be used to benefit humanity. They are committed to studying ancient civilizations, conducting exobiology research on planets, and constantly updating the galactic map with new planets and stars. The team is also actively involved in politics, playing a significant role in the intricate power dynamics of the universe.

The Scientific Assembly is comprised of talented members from the Empire, Federation, and Alliance, all residing in the Indri system. At the helm of this esteemed group of scientists is Harrison Wells.
