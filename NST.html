Nova Technocratic Scientific Assembly


Nova Science Team is a team of Imperial scientists and explorers of Nova Navy ruled by Nova Technocratic Scientific Assembly. Formed in 3307 as Branch of Auxiliary in Nova Navy they gained enough resources and support from Nova Paresa and local Factions to raise as own faction with own settlements, cities and starports under Nova Navy jurisdiction.

Their task, is to study the mysteries of the galaxy and bring new knowledge to humanity about the endless emptiness of space. They are engaged in the study of the ancient civilizations and are engaged in exobiology on planets, update the galmap with new planets and stars. And ofcourse politics. Scientific Assembly consist of members from Empire, Federation and Alliance in Indri system, General Speaker of Assembly is Harrison Wells, the founder of Nova Science Team in 3307 as branch of Nova Navy's Auxiliary then expanded as self-governable faction under Nova Navy jurisdiction.
